FIAT - Driven by passion

The perfect solution for a big city

01. Model presentation

The design of this brand is the embodiment of a harmonious combination of modern technologies, environmental friendliness and exquisite appearance. The 500e is not much different from its predecessor with internal combustion engines (internal combustion engine), but the changes can be noticed immediately: the rear of the car has a wider spoiler, and the air intake grille is not shaped like a smile.

02. Minimalist design

The striking, emotional, dynamic appearance of the Fiat 500e won the hearts of many users. Externally, the FIAT 500E practically does not differ from its gasoline brother, with the exception of some details: the air intake grille, specific rims, bumpers and an aerodynamic spoiler. This added a special zest to the look of the Italian and you can be sure that many will turn to throw another look after the FIAT 500E.

03. Car interior

The casing is made of materials of the highest quality, pleasant to the touch. The combination of white, black and orange perfectly emphasizes the advantages of the interior and its sophistication. The downside is that there is a battery in the back of the car, so only children or very short people can feel comfortable in the back seat. But on the two front seats there is enough space for a convenient location of the passenger and the driver, regardless of their height and configuration.

04. Specifications

The power unit of the Fiat 500e is on the floor, the car's platform has been reduced, and the specific engine power is 111 horsepower or 83 kW, the torque is 199 Nm. The casing is made of materials of the highest quality, pleasant to the touch. The combination of white, black and orange perfectly emphasizes the advantages of the interior and its sophistication.

05. Charging & Battery

The electric motor is powered by a 24 kWh lithium-ion battery. The battery is divided into ninety-seven cells. Charging is done with a 6.6 kW charger (SAE standard). A fully discharged battery from a standard network can be recharged in just 4 hours. Charge status information is always displayed on the instrument panel.

The Fiat 500e does not have a fast charging function. The manufacturer claims that thanks to the braking energy recovery system, it is possible to recover some of the energy spent during the movement. This technology increases the mileage per charging cycle (up to 129 km on the highway, and up to 160 km in urban areas).

The casing is made of materials of the highest quality, pleasant to the touch. The combination of white, black and orange perfectly emphasizes the advantages of the interior and its sophistication. The downside is that there is a battery in the back of the car, so only children or very short people can feel comfortable in the back seat. But on the two front seats there is enough space for a convenient location of the passenger and the driver, regardless of their height and configuration.

07. Car Benefits

Although a car powered by electricity is an order of magnitude quieter than analogs with an internal combustion engine, this was not enough for the developers, they equipped the Fiat 500e with a sound-absorbing panel, special mats, a new windshield and modernized door seals. Especially for this model, the programmers have developed a unique application for smartphones, thanks to which you can view statistics on energy consumption for previous trips from a distance, monitor the state of the car, turn on / off charging, plan your route so that there are definitely charging stations along the way.
